# Agents and Skills
## Talk Slides

---

## Quote

> "Great systems are built by combining clear goals, fast feedback, and reusable skills."

---

## Quote

> "Agents are most useful when they reduce repeat work and increase confidence in decisions."

---

## Focus Areas

- Clear problem framing before action
- Small, testable increments
- Reusable skills over one-off scripts
- Progress tracking with visible checkpoints

---

## Focus Areas by Outcome

- **Speed:** parallelized exploration and automation
- **Quality:** validation loops and safe defaults
- **Clarity:** consistent planning and traceable outputs
- **Learning:** dated milestones and reflective notes

---

## Closing Prompt

- What skill can you package once and reuse in five future tasks?
